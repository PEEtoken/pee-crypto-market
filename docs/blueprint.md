# **App Name**: PEE Cripto Market

## Core Features:

- Hero Section & Call to Action: Display an impactful hero section with the main title, subtitle, suggested modern supermarket imagery with crypto interfaces, and the primary call to action 'Activar mi Wallet de Fundador - 1 USDT'.
- Dynamic Founding Wallets Counter: Show a real-time counter of 'Founding Wallets' to demonstrate community growth and transparency, pulling data from an external source.
- Multisig Security and Blockchain Transparency: Present detailed information about the 6/4 multisig wallet security, ensuring collective community control and the immutable registration of founder wallets on the blockchain.
- Interactive 24-Month Roadmap: Visualize the project's development phases (Comunidad, Mercado Digital, Supermercado Físico) in an interactive, vertical timeline, allowing users to understand the strategic growth.
- TON Wallets FAQ: Provide a clear and concise section answering frequently asked questions about using TON-compatible wallets (Tonkeeper/Telegram) for newcomers to DeFi.
- PEE Token Utility and Value: Clearly outline the benefits and value proposition of the PEE Token, including exclusive discounts, loyalty programs, and governance rights within the ecosystem.
- Founder Wallet Activation & Registration Tool: Enable users to securely connect their TON wallet, initiate the 1 USDT contribution, and register as a founder, with an AI tool providing context-aware guidance and personalized confirmation messages throughout the blockchain transaction process.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light color scheme, designed for approachability and modernity. Primary accent color is a deep, trustworthy blue (#24478F), symbolizing innovation and community. Background is a very light, almost white cool grey (#F3F4F6) for cleanliness and focus. The accent color for interactive elements and highlights is a vibrant, clear cyan (#3CC2DD), denoting energy and digital fluidity.
- Headlines will use 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, tech-forward, yet legible appearance. Body text will utilize 'Inter' (sans-serif) for optimal readability and a neutral, professional tone, suitable for beginners in DeFi.
- Adopt a style of clean, minimalistic line-art or flat icons that subtly integrate elements of Web3, cryptocurrency interfaces, and supermarket imagery. Visual integration with the TON network will be emphasized through a consistent use of relevant symbols without clutter.
- The layout will be clean, modular, and responsive, with clearly delineated sections for easy navigation. Emphasis will be on visual hierarchy to guide users through the information, particularly an 'impactful Hero' and an interactive vertical roadmap. A mobile-first approach ensures accessibility for all users.
- Subtle, fluid animations and transitions will be employed to enhance user experience, such as a dynamic counter for founding wallets and interactive elements in the roadmap. These animations will be designed to emphasize the speed and efficiency of the TON network.
